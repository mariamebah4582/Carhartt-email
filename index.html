The Originals

A Carhartt WIP Throwback

Seen here on Detroit rapper, singer and producer Illa J – younger brother of J Dilla and former member of the seminal Slum Village collective – is a selection of new-old pieces. For F/W18, Carhartt WIP has dug into its archive, to recreate the iconic Michigan Chore Coat in its O.G. fit – a little more boxy and oversized than is typical. The Bib Overall has received similar treatment, and is also executed in a washed duck canvas. It’s a throwback to when clothes were built a little looser, and all the more current for it.
